Interests and hobbies

Besides coding I have lots of interests and hobbies outside the tech world which I will try to briefly describe here. In this section, I try to explain the hobby and why I am keen on it.

Formula 1

I love fast cars. And if you combine it with a championship, I'm in ecstasy. Remember watching it with my dad when I was little. When things happen on the track I cannot stop myself from watching it.

Parkour

This hobby I picked up in recent years. It's an art of getting from one place to another in the most complicated ways possible and I'm in love with it. My friends and I try to find spots all around our city.

Video Games

As long as I can remember me having a computer, I've always loved games. I was amazed by the graphics and mechanics of GTA Vice City. Even nowadays, I spend some time playing video games.

Photography

I cannot say that I'm a big enthusiast of photography, I don't have professional equipment but I admire this idea of capturing the moment which later could be recalled as memories.

Video Editing

Telling a story is an art. And it doesn't matter what equipment do you have, editing is what makes video tell a good story. I'm not a big fan of editing itself but I like looking at the final product.

Electric Skateboards

I'm fan of idea of a last-mile transportation. So in 2019, I bought myself an electric skateboard. Till now, it was the best purchase I've ever made. It's not just a fun way of transportation but also, crazy fast.
